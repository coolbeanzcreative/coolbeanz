institute for hormonal health

client project role
Love on Sunday Branding and visual identity for a Canadian integrative medical clinic Brand Designer

background

The Institute for Hormonal Health (IHH) is the premier integrative medical clinic in Canada specializing in the treatment of health issues that are hormonal in origin. The Institute utilizes stateof-the-art technology and an unparalleled team of medical professionals to provide the highest level of care. Toronto design studio Love on Sunday asked us to create a brand style guide for IHH to complement their existing logo.

logo

The IHH logo should be used on all aspects of IHH branded communication. Together, the symbol and wordmark create the IHH logo and should not be altered, tilted, distorted, manipulated or disassembled on any application

color palette

Color is a primary means of visual identifcation that we use to create a powerful emotional response. Our colors were chosen with care to convey that we are a vibrant, forward- looking brand. The consistent use of a limited number of colors will build strong external recognition and memorability for Six Canyon.

primary palette

The Primary Palette consists of three Greens, which are intended to be the main signifying colors for the brand. They should be used for all solid fill graphics.

secondary palette

The Secondary Palette consists of Green #4, Brown and Cool Grey which are supporting colors that provide a foundation for our Primary Palette to stand out. Cool Grey can be used as a background color.

gradient

The IHH Gradient is created from a blend of the Primary Palette. This gradient adds a sense of depth and texture to both the IHH logo and other graphics. In terms of creating and using the gradient, there should always be a subtle feel to the color transition across a page or graphic.
