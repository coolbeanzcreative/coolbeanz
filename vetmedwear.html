VetMedWear Logo and packaging design for a veterinary medicine company Graphic Designer

background

VetMedWear was started with one mission: to provide comfort, mobility, and reduce stress for animals after their surgery by eliminating the need to use an E-Collar. The VetMedWear Gown is a patented post-operative dressing designed to protect abdominal wounds from scratching, biting, or licking medicine. Itâ€™s a reliable and effective after surgery wear solution that meets the highest standards of veterinary medicine. VetMedWear approached us to design their logo and packaging for their catalogue of products.

logo


VetMedWear wanted a simple logo that clearly represented their company in the veterinary medicine space. Their line of products provides health benefits to recovering pets, allowing them to heal quicker and enjoy their days without worrying about their surgery. We represented this in their logo by placing an arc above a large dog in positive space and a smaller cat in negative space, symbolizing shelter and life of all pets, big and small.


packaging

The packages for the products are sealed bags to hold the garments within. We wanted the design to quickly communicate the type of product on the front side and instructions and sizing charts on the back side. All of the text had to be shown in English and French. Our final design showcases an image of the product worn on a pet model on the front, and organized instructions, diagrams and sizing charts on the back.
